Anyone Can Be an Entrepeneur

From a quote on Quora... 

An idea is not a mockup
A mockup is not a prototype 
A prototype is not a program 
A program is not a product 
A product is not a business 
And a business is not profits

...nSeption was conceived.

Many are looking to take their idea from being sketches on a napkin, to being a money maker. The problem is that how to get there can seem overwhelming as many, including myself, are not sure how to get there.

nSeption tracks your ideas, mockups, prototyping, program development lifecycle, product design and marketing, and business formation and planning to selling your product. nSeption helps you to stay organized and on track to turning your ideas into money-making realities. nSeption also gives you the information you need in each phase of the process to stay on track.

nSeption is for those taking and idea to making a profit for the first time or for those who need a tool to help track and monitor the progress of each idea. Whether you're on your desktop, tablet or smartphone, you will always by in sync with the progress of your ideas. Does an idea come to you and you need to write it down? Use any of your devices to type or draw your idea to your backlog of ideas your are tracking.

Prototyping

Prototyping is easy with nSeption. Whether you want to use the built-in tooling to create your wireframes, import from your favorite wireframing tool, or you prefer to draw them with your touch device. Your prototypes are linked to your ideas along with suggest timelines. Not every idea will make profits. A good rule of thumb is that if you can't develop the program based on your idea within two weeks, then the idea is too complex.

How Do I Setup My Business?

Setting up a business to sell your product can be an undertaking in itself. nSeption will guide you through the process to being a limited liability, S-Corporation or fully incorporated. With links to many resources you will be able to find the information you need and will track your progress to getting your business setup quickly.
